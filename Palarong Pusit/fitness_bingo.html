Mechanics


The fifth game is called FITNESS BINGO. In this game, as the title implies, each player will be given a bingo card with different corresponding exercises in it. The bingo card will include its own workout, which must be completed within a certain period. Participants will start their workouts starting from “P” until they reach the last exercise at “T.” If the participants are not able to comply, they will be eliminated. To guarantee that each participant has completed the activity, they will be required to provide a video proof of completion.

There will only be 2 winners for the final round. The first two people to submit their entry will be the announced winner and will have a chance to win a cash prize.

If you are recently vaccinated this is the mechanics for you.

About Us

We are the Grade 11-Faith, the founder of this event. “Palarong Pusit" is a competitive and free-for-all like "Squid Game", but with a twist. Every game consists of a variety of exercises designed to get everyone moving and sweating.
