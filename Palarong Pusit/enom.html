Mechanics


The forth game is called ENOM (Every Number on the Minute). During the registration, you have given us an ENOM (every number on the minute) of your choice. Since there are three (3) numbers in every player’s ID, every player will get the foresaid number of minutes to each assigned exercise and rest according to their ID. The time limit for the first exercise will depend on the first number of the player’s ID. The time limit for the rest/pause time of the participant will depend on the second number of the player’s ID and so on. If one of the player’s ID numbers is zero (0), the length of time they have would be (10) minutes.

Ex. ENOM (Player ID) is 329, then the 1st exercise will be 3 mins, the rest time will be 2 mins, while the 3rd exercise will be 9 mins.

Only a maximum of 10 participants will be able to proceed to the next round, so submit your entry early and do your best to proceed to the next round.

If you are recently vaccinated this is the mechanics for you.
