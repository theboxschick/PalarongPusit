Mechanics

The first game is called Red Light, Yellow Light, and Green Light. When you see and hear the words “red light”, you are not permitted to move, you will be eliminated if you are caught moving. However, when you see and hear “green light”, you will perform either push-ups or squats. On the other hand, when you see and hear “yellow light”, you will be given 30 seconds to rest (along the game, you will see and hear “yellow light” 2 times). If you can survive the 5-minute game, you will automatically proceed to the next round. However, if the participants are caught moving during the red light, they will immediately be eliminated. Only a maximum of 30 participants will be able to proceed to the next round, so submit your entry early and do your best to proceed to the next round.

If you are recently vaccinated this is the mechanics for you.
Alternative Mechanics

Alternative Mechanics

The first game is called Red Light, Yellow Light, and Green Light. When you see and hear “red light”, it indicates that you are not permitted to move (balance yourself). You will be eliminated if you are caught moving. When you see and hear “green light”, however, you will perform jog in place. On the other hand, when you see and hear “yellow light”, you will be given 30 seconds to rest (along the game, you will see and hear “yellow light” 2 times).
