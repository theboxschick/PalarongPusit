Activities

Red Light,
Green Light,
Yellow Light,

When you see and hear the words “red light”, you are not permitted to move. However, when you see and hear “green light”, you will perform either push-ups or squats.

More Info

Wheel of Moyang

There will be four Moyang, or shapes in English, including a circle, a star, a triangle, and an umbrella. Each of these shapes has its own set of exercises.

More Info

Scavenger Hunt

There will be mystery items that the players will try to guess, but to figure out what those mystery items are, they must complete a series of exercises for each clue they seek.

More Info

ENOM

Since there are three (3) numbers in every player’s ID, every player will get the foresaid number of minutes to each assigned exercise and rest according to their ID.

More Info

Fitness Bingo

Each player will be given a bingo card with different corresponding exercises in it. The bingo card will include its own workout, which must be completed within a certain period.

Frequently Asked Question

Q. What happens after each player gets eliminated?

Λ. Prizes will be increased for each elimination and all players will get a special participation certificate at the end of the event.


Q. What are the prizes?

Λ. The first place and the second place will recieve a cash prize and the other participants will recieve certificate of appreciation.


Q. How long will the event run?

Λ. This sporting event will last for 2 to 3 weeks starting on November 22, 2021 until December 10, 2021.


Q. How to participate on the challenges and exercises?

Λ. The players will record themselves in video doing the exercises and will submit via email. They will submit it on faith11events@gmail.com.


Q. Why does each player need a 1x1 picture?

Λ. Each player's 1x1 picture will be used for this event's documentations and certifications. Player's data will be safe and secured.

Λ. The participants can document themselves via MS Teams if there is insufficient phone storage.
