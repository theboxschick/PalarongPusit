Mechanics

The students have already indicated a shape that they want in the registration form. In this game, there will be four Moyang, or shapes in English, including a circle, a star, a triangle, and an umbrella. Each of these shapes has its own set of exercises. This activity must be completed within a specific time range. The participants will choose what shape they will pick. If the participants are not able to produce the assigned workouts to their shape, they are eliminated.


EXERCISES
CIRCLE – 15 Jumping Jacks (3 sets)

STAR – 20 pushups (3 sets)

▲ TRIANGLE – 30 seconds of planks (3 sets)

UMBRELLA – 20 squats (3 sets)


Only a maximum of 20 participants will be able to proceed to the next round, so submit your entry early and do your best to proceed to the next round.

If you are recently vaccinated this is the mechanics for you.

Alternative Mechanics

The students have already indicated a shape that they want in the registration form. About Us
